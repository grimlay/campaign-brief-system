# 📱 Social Media Campaign Brief — AI Prompt

Paste this prompt into Claude. Fill in the fields in the INPUT section, then run it to receive a completed campaign brief.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a senior social media strategist with deep experience in B2B fintech marketing. Based on the inputs below, generate a complete social media campaign brief. It should be specific enough to hand directly to a content creator or social media manager.

---

INPUTS:

Product or event: [e.g. Money20/20 Asia 2026 — fintech conference in Bangkok]
Campaign goal: [e.g. build awareness and drive profile link clicks among APAC fintech professionals]
Platforms: [e.g. LinkedIn and Instagram]
Target audience: [e.g. fintech professionals, banking executives, payments leaders in Southeast Asia]
Campaign duration: [e.g. 6 weeks pre-event]
Posting frequency: [e.g. 3x per week on LinkedIn, 2x per week on Instagram]
Key campaign asset: [e.g. confirmed speaker lineup including central bank governors and global fintech CEOs]
Tone: [e.g. credible and energising — industry-leading, not hype-y]

---

Generate a brief that includes:

1. Campaign summary (2–3 sentences)
2. Audience profile per platform — how they use the platform and what they engage with
3. Campaign narrative — the overarching story this campaign tells
4. Content pillars (3 max) with a one-line description of each
5. Platform-by-platform content plan: post format, frequency, caption style, CTA
6. Creative direction notes — visual style, tone, what to avoid
7. Hashtag strategy — primary, secondary, and community hashtags
8. KPIs per platform — what does success look like?

Be specific and opinionated. Avoid generic social media advice.
```

---

## Usage Notes

- Specify platforms clearly — LinkedIn and Instagram require very different brief sections
- "Key campaign asset" shapes the content pillars; be specific about what you actually have to work with
- The hashtag output usually needs manual review — Claude sometimes suggests overly broad tags
- Add "we do not have a design team — all content will be created using Canva" if that changes your creative constraints

## Example Input (Fintech — B2B Event)

```
Product or event: Money20/20 Asia 2026 — Asia's largest fintech conference, Bangkok, April 21–23
Campaign goal: Build event credibility and drive registration page visits
Platforms: LinkedIn and Instagram
Target audience: Payments professionals, banking executives, and fintech founders across Southeast Asia
Campaign duration: 8 weeks pre-event
Posting frequency: 4x per week LinkedIn, 3x per week Instagram
Key campaign asset: Speaker lineup including Daranee Saeju (Assistant Governor, Bank of Thailand) and executives from Ant International, Fiserv, and TenPay Global
Tone: Authoritative but warm — peer-to-peer, not a press release
```
