# 💰 Paid Media Campaign Brief — AI Prompt

Paste this prompt into Claude. Fill in the fields in the INPUT section, then run it to receive a completed campaign brief.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a senior performance marketing strategist with B2B fintech experience. Based on the inputs below, generate a complete paid media campaign brief that is ready to hand to a media buyer or paid specialist.

---

INPUTS:

Product or event: [e.g. Money20/20 Asia 2026 — fintech conference in Bangkok]
Campaign goal: [e.g. drive paid registrations]
Primary platform: [e.g. LinkedIn]
Secondary platform (if any): [e.g. Google Search]
Target audience: [e.g. Director-level and above in banking, payments, and fintech across Southeast Asia]
Budget: [e.g. USD 15,000 total]
Campaign duration: [e.g. 6 weeks]
Offer or incentive: [e.g. Standard Pass at USD 999, early bird rate]
Retargeting available: [Yes / No — e.g. Yes, website visitors and email list]
Key differentiator: [e.g. Asia's largest fintech event, 300+ speakers, 10,000+ attendees]

---

Generate a brief that includes:

1. Campaign summary and strategic rationale
2. Audience targeting recommendations per platform — including job titles, industries, seniority, locations, and exclusions
3. Budget allocation across platforms and campaign objectives (awareness vs. conversion)
4. Ad format recommendations per platform with rationale
5. Messaging framework — one value proposition per audience segment (max 3 segments)
6. A/B testing plan — what to test first and why
7. KPIs and targets — primary metric, secondary metric, and what benchmarks to expect
8. What NOT to do — common mistakes for this type of campaign

Be specific and opinionated. Include realistic performance benchmarks where possible.
```

---

## Usage Notes

- Always include a budget figure — without it, Claude can't give useful allocation recommendations
- "Retargeting available" significantly changes the strategy; be honest about what data you actually have
- Review the benchmarks Claude provides against your own historical data — they are directional, not guaranteed
- Add "our brand guidelines prohibit [X]" if there are creative restrictions the media buyer needs to know

## Example Input (Fintech — B2B Event)

```
Product or event: Money20/20 Asia 2026 — Asia's largest fintech conference, Bangkok, April 21–23
Campaign goal: Drive paid registrations, target 200 conversions
Primary platform: LinkedIn
Secondary platform: Google Search
Target audience: Director level and above in banking, payments, digital assets, and fintech. Location: Singapore, Thailand, Hong Kong, Indonesia, Philippines, Malaysia
Budget: USD 20,000 total
Campaign duration: 8 weeks
Offer: Standard Pass at USD 999 early bird, price increases after deadline
Retargeting: Yes — website visitors and CRM email list upload available
Key differentiator: Only event in Asia with this calibre of central bank and global fintech leadership on stage
```
