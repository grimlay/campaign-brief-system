# 📋 Campaign Brief Template System

A system for generating structured, ready-to-use campaign briefs using AI — built for fintech marketers who need to move fast without sacrificing strategic clarity.

Each folder contains two things:
1. **A blank template** — fill it in manually when you have all the information
2. **An AI prompt** — paste into Claude with minimal input and get a full brief generated for you

---

## Why this exists

Campaign briefs are often either too vague ("make it engaging") or too long to actually be useful. This system sits in the middle — structured enough to align stakeholders, concise enough that people actually read it.

Built from real campaign work in fintech, B2B events, and financial services marketing.

---

## How to use it

**Option A — Manual**
1. Open the template file for your campaign type
2. Fill in each field
3. Share with your team or agency

**Option B — AI-generated**
1. Open the prompt file for your campaign type
2. Paste the prompt into [Claude](https://claude.ai)
3. Answer the input fields with your campaign details
4. Get a completed brief in under 60 seconds
5. Review, edit, and share
